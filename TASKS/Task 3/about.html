About Us

Accounts Hub is one of the leading institutions in Kerala which provides training in many job oriented IT courses. It is founded by highly experienced teachers in the field of finance, Accounting and Taxation. Our staff has the experience of more than 20 years in the industry. We provide personal attention to each student to get maximum practical knowledge in their courses. Our faculties make learning easier for all levels of students. Our institution has earned a remarkable fame by its past experience which shows a growing rate. We also do accounting and GST works of different firms according to their needs. Our institution also provides distance education courses (SSLC, Plus Two, BA, B.COM, MA. M COM,MBA). We are giving quality education and anyone can access it through our various batches. Through these programs, learners Can complete their academic education and professional goals which enhance employability and career prospects. Our training covers manual and computerized accounting and it will improve the ability of the candidate and he is able to deal with different areas. The Institution provides the students with standard professional and linguistic training to improve their potential and interpersonal skills. We also promote placement assistance to all the candidates after the successful completion of the course.
