POST GRADUATION COURSES

PG courses (Postgraduate courses) refer to advanced academic programs that lead to the award of a master's degree, typically lasting two years, in a specific field of study, after completing a bachelor's degree.

Master of Arts

(MA - 2 years)

MA (Master of Arts) is a postgraduate degree course that focuses on humanities, social sciences, and liberal arts, such as English, History, Psychology, Sociology, and other related fields.

Master of Commerce

(M.Com - 2 years)

MCom (Master of Commerce) is a postgraduate degree course that focuses on commerce, accounting, finance, and business management, providing advanced knowledge and skills in areas such as financial management, accounting, marketing, and human resources.

Master of Business Administration

(MBA - 2 years)

A Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate degree that focuses on developing advanced business skills, knowledge, and practices.
