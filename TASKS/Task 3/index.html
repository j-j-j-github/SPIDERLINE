ABOUT ACCOUNTS HUB

Accounts Hub is one of the leading institutions in Kerala offering job-oriented IT, finance, accounting, and taxation courses. Founded by experienced professionals with over 20 years in the industry, we provide personal attention and practical training for students of all levels. We also offer accounting and GST services for firms along with distance education courses such as SSLC, Plus Two, BA, B.Com, MA, M.Com, and MBA. Through our quality education and flexible batches, students can achieve their academic and professional goals while improving career opportunities.
